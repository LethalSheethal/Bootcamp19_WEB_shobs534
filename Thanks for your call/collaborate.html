Thanks for your call

A collaborative contact center role playing game.

Goal

Solve as many problems as you can as quickly as you can and get good reviews by customers. Don't worry. Your friends (even virtual ones) will help you. Don't stress.

Characters

Customer - aka The Patient Requestor

  • Professional
  • Friendly
  • Wants their issue to be resolved efficiently

Agent - aka The Problem Resolver

  • Art Student
  • Loves solving problems
  • Likes talking to people

AI - aka Agent Assistant, (played by me)

  • Interpreter
  • Solves problems before they become problems
  • Makes the system more human

Real Time Analyst - aka Conductor (optional)

  • Musician
  • Calm and composed
  • Loves making life easier for people around them

Rules

Customer - to advance to the next level, you need to help the agent advance to the next level.

  • You explain your issue to the AI assistant when you call
  • Your AI friend interprets your issue and helps the agent solve the problem quickly
  • Patience is rewarded. Screaming is not.
  • (Expansion set focuses on empathy building as customers. Stay tuned)

Agent - to advance to the next level, you need to solve as many problems as you can as fast as you can.

  • The customer will explain their issue to the AI assistant when they call
  • The AI will interpret the issue and sends you relevant info to solve the prolbem more quickly
  • You can consult your AI assistant at any time as you discuss the issue with the customer

AI Agent Assistant - to advance to the next level, you need to make the agent feel as calm as you can while helping them solve issues.

  • The customer explains their issue to the AI assistant when they call
  • AI assistant interprets the issue and sends the agent relevant info to solve the problem more quickly
  • The agent and the Real Time Analyst can consult you for help at any time for help. However,you help them before they reach out to you.

Real Time Analyst - to advance to the next level, you need to ensure there is enough staff and AI assistants to cover the lines and keep the agents calm.

  • As customers call in, you should ensure the wait is no longer than 2mins
  • AI assistant can hep you too
  • The agent can consult you at any time for help.
